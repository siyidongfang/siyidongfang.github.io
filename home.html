Curriculum Vitae

Welcome! I'm a PhD student working with Prof. Andrew Stewart at UCLA.

I study the dynamics of ocean circulation around the Antarctic margins using a combination of theories, numerical models, and in-situ data. My research interests lie broadly in geophysical fluid dynamics, turbulence, ice-ocean interactions, eddies, and tides on Earth and other ocean worlds.

Education:

    • 2018-present, Ph.D. Candidate in Physical Oceanography, UCLA

    • 2014-2018, B.S. in Astronomy, School of Physics, Peking University
